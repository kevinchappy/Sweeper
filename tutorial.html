Welcome

So you wanna be a Minesweeper ? Well you have come to the right place. In this tutorial we will go over the basic controls and interface of Minesweeper. So strap in and pay attention cadet!

The Rules

Minesweeper is a game with clickable tiles, where some of these tiles have mines hidden behind them. The goal is to reveal all tiles while avoiding the mines. Importantly, your focus should be on trying to deduce where mines are using logic, not by guessing.

The Controls

The basic controls are:

Left Click
Reveals a tile. If the tile has a mine you lose.
Right Click
Places a flag on a tile that you think contains a mine. Alternatively, toggle flag placement mode with the flag button at the bottom of the screen if your device does not have a right click button

The Numbers

When you reveal a tile without a mine you might see a number displayed. This number signifies the amount of mines adjacent to the revealed tile. You can use this information to deduce where mines are located. For example, consider the image to the right. The flagged tile must have a mine since it is the only unrevealed tile next to the 1 tile. Also, there must be two other mines in the un-clicked tiles to the left because of the adjacent 3 tile.

The Buttons

On the game board you will see some buttons. These will help you access different features and settings. They are as follows:

  • Smiley button - Restarts the game.
  • Flag button - Toggles flag placement mode.
  • Settings button - Shows settings menu.
  • Audio button - toggles background music on/off.
